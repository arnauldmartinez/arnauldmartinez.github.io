Guidance, Navigation, and Control Intern, SpaceX

Mr. James Dearman, Dr. Jon Herman | Redmond, WA June 2024 – September 2024

I worked under Dr. Herman and Mr. Dearman to build a physics simulation that captures the physical state of the entire Starlink satellite fleet. I built and optimized the simulation in C++ and fetched/serialized real time telemetry data in Python. I integrated custom rk4 solvers that propagate the ephemeride states of the satellites forward, accurate to ~2 weeks. I also developed models for on-board batteries and antennae, with the ability to bootstrap to a future time and simulate various failure modes.
